Mapping Police Complaints

Visualizing hotspots of complaints
about Chicago police by beat

Now what do we have here

The Civilian Office of Police Accountability (COPA), an independent organization within the City of Chicago, collects and processes civilian complaints about police. Below you will find a series of spatially corelated area maps of Police Beats with disproprotionately high numbers of complaints by the race of the person making the complaint.

This is the start of project to quantify and visualize the civilian side of police interactions.
